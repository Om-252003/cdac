1. \w

Matches word characters.

Equivalent to: [A-Za-z0-9_]

Includes:

Uppercase letters (A–Z)

Lowercase letters (a–z)

Digits (0–9)

Underscore (_)


2. \D

Matches non-digit characters (opposite of \d).

Equivalent to: [^0-9]

Includes:

Letters (A–Z, a–z)

Underscore (_)

Spaces, punctuation, special symbols, etc.
